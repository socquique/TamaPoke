🐢 TamaPoke

A 151-Pokémon tamagotchi for the Waveshare ESP32-S3-Touch-AMOLED-1.75. Install it from your browser — no Arduino, no drivers.

⚠️ Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Use Chrome or Edge on a computer.

1Flash the firmware

Plug the board in via USB and press install. Pick the port and wait.

Browser not supported (use Chrome/Edge). Needs HTTPS.

🔄 Updating to a new version? Install without erasing to keep your Pokémon — updates flash only the firmware parts and leave the save area untouched. Only a full Erase wipes your saved pet. First time on a fresh board? You can erase, no problem.

2Load the sprites onto the microSD

Put a microSD into the board (it formats itself). Press Connect board and then Load sprites: they download and copy over USB automatically (~40 MB, a few minutes). Don't remove the card.

Close the step-1 install tab first (only one program can use the port at a time).

Have your own .bin files?

🎨 Want to make your own sprites? See how to generate & load them in the repo.


  


  
How to play

  
Care for your egg until it hatches, feed it, play, train it and complete them
     all. Tap the creature to pet it; swipe to open the Pokédex (sideways), the
     stat card (up) or set the clock (down); the physical PWR button turns the
     screen off (short press) or everything off (long press).
     More in the repository.
